About Us

Our History & Mission

Galewela Pradeshiya Sabha has been serving the community for over three decades. Established in 1990, we have consistently worked towards the development and welfare of our region and its people.

Our mission is to provide efficient public services, foster economic growth, and maintain the cultural heritage of Galewela while ensuring sustainable development for future generations.

We are committed to transparency, accountability, and community participation in all our initiatives. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to address the needs of our citizens and improve their quality of life.
